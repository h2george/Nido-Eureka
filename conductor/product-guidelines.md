# Product Guidelines: eureka-nido---guardería

- **Voice and Tone**: Friendly and approachable (Dirigido a padres de familia)
- **Design Principles**: 
  - Simplicity over features
  - Nurturing Modernism (Round, warm, professional)
  - Accessibility (Contrast and clarity)
- **Naming Conventions**: PascalCase for components, camelCase for functions and variables.
- **Language**: Español (Interface), Inglés (Codebase)
